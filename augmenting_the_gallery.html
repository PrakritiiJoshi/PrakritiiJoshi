  • Man-Computer Symbiosis, J. C. R. Licklider, IRE Transactions on Human Factors in Electronics, volume HFE-1, 1960.

  • The Ultimate Display, Ivan Sutherland, Proceedings of IFIP congress, 1965.
  • A Survey of Augmented Reality, Rick van Krevelen and Ronald Poelman, Delft University, 2010.

  • This reading discusses the history of the technology Augmented Reality and the different applications and limitations. Augmented reality helps us see, hear, and feel our environment in new and enriched ways. The definition of AR is described as combining real and virtual in a real environment, registering(aligning) real and virtual objects with each other, and as three dimensional, interactive, and in real time. Mediated or diminished reality is removing real objects by overlaying virtual ones.

    • Assignment

    Prompt- Develop a Unity application which tells a story about an object (a 3D model) through text and images. Use at least 4 text fields and 4 images. Make your story change over time by writing a timer script which will change the content of at least one image and/or one text field. For example, after 10 seconds, an image could switch from the picture of a water fountain to a water bottle. You can choose to be as explicit or as you want about how the information relates to the object
